My Cat Pepper

Pepper is a grey and white cat that my wife and I adopted on August 13th, 2023. My aunt's co-worker found him alone in the woods in her backyard and took him in when he was just a few weeks old. She took him in and bottle-fed him but he didn't get along with the other cat in the house so he needed to be re-homed when he was around 3 months old. He's the best guy.

Admittedly, it took time for us to get used to having Pepper but we learned about "The Rule of Threes" when getting your first pet:

Time Frame Vibe Description
3 Days This is too hard and the worst decision we've ever made.
3 Weeks This is still really hard but we can see how it could be okay.
3 Months We can't imagine our lives without the pet.

Pepper's Likes and Dislikes

  • Likes
    • Running back and forth through the apartment

  • Dislikes

    • Going on car trips
